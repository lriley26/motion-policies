Privacy Policy for Motion.ai

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

Introduction

Motion.ai ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, password
  • Profile Information: Age, weight, height, gender, activity level
  • Activity Data: Activities you log, duration, intensity, calories burned
  • Goals: Weekly calorie burn targets you set

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features you use
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Log Data: Crash reports, error logs

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the Motion.ai service
  • Calculate personalized calorie burn estimates
  • Track your progress toward fitness goals
  • Send you notifications about your activities
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Improve app features and user experience

Third-Party Services

Mistral AI (Activity Classification)

We use Mistral AI to help classify and process activity descriptions you enter. When you describe an activity (e.g., "I ran for 30 minutes"), this text is sent to Mistral AI's servers for processing.

What we send to Mistral AI:

  • Your activity descriptions (text only)
  • No personal information, names, or identifying details

What we don't send:

  • Email address, name, or account information
  • Location data
  • Health metrics or historical data
  • Any information beyond the activity description

Data retention:

  • Mistral AI does not store your input after processing
  • Responses are used only to classify your activity

Purpose:

  • To automatically detect activity type, duration, and intensity
  • To provide faster and easier activity logging

For more information: https://mistral.ai/terms/

Supabase (Database & Authentication)

We use Supabase to securely store your account and activity data.

What we store:

  • Your account credentials (encrypted)
  • Profile information
  • Activity logs and goals
  • App preferences

Security: All data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

For more information: https://supabase.com/privacy

RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

If you purchase a subscription, RevenueCat processes your payment information.

What RevenueCat receives:

  • Purchase receipts from Apple/Google
  • No credit card information (handled by Apple/Google)

For more information: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to anyone.

We only share data with:

  • Mistral AI (activity descriptions for classification)
  • Supabase (to store your data securely)
  • RevenueCat (to manage subscriptions)
  • Apple/Google (for app distribution and payments)

Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: Export your activity logs
  • Delete your data: Request account deletion
  • Correct your data: Update profile information
  • Opt-out of AI processing: Use manual activity entry instead

To exercise your rights: Contact us at tolarcorp@gmail.com

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Activity logs: Stored until you delete them or close your account

Children's Privacy

Motion.ai is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information we collect
  • Right to request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)

To exercise your California rights: Contact us at tolarcorp@gmail.com

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Legal basis for processing:

  • Performance of contract (to provide app services)
  • Legitimate interests (to improve the app)
  • Consent (for optional features like AI classification)

To exercise your EU rights: Contact us at tolarcorp@gmail.com

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest (database encryption)
  • Secure authentication (password hashing)
  • Regular security updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Showing an in-app notification
  • Sending an email (for major changes)

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
